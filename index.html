FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Have more questions? Our AI student assistant is available 24/7 — just click the button in the bottom right.

How do I enroll at MedBridge Academy?
You can enroll online or visit us in person. You'll need to submit a completed application, a valid government-issued ID, and a high school diploma or GED. Once reviewed, you'll be invited to attend a mandatory orientation before signing your enrollment agreement.
What is the refund policy?
Students who withdraw within 3 days of signing their enrollment agreement receive a full refund. Withdrawals after 3 days but before the program start date are subject to a $150 administrative fee. No refund is issued after the program begins unless special circumstances are reviewed and approved by administration.
What is the cancellation policy?
Students may cancel enrollment by submitting a written cancellation request. Cancellations made after attending orientation will forfeit the registration fee. Please contact our admissions office as early as possible if you need to cancel.
How long are the programs?
Both the Medical Assistant and Dental Assistant programs are 9 months long, including a 160-hour externship in the final month at a partnered clinic or dental office.
What are the class schedule options?
Classes run Monday through Friday. We offer morning cohorts starting at 8:00 AM and evening cohorts starting at 5:00 PM, depending on availability at your campus location.
What certification exams will I be prepared for?
Medical Assistant graduates are prepared to sit for the NHA Certified Clinical Medical Assistant (CCMA) exam. Dental Assistant graduates are prepared for the DANB Certified Dental Assistant (CDA) exam. Exam prep is included in your program curriculum.
Is financial aid available?
Yes. MedBridge Academy accepts federal financial aid through FAFSA. Flexible payment plans are also available. Our financial aid advisors will work with you to find the best option for your situation.
How does the externship work?
All students complete a 160-hour externship at a partnered clinic or dental office during their final month. MedBridge Academy coordinates placement on your behalf, so you don't need to find your own site.
